Areas I am focusing on
Urgent

Two websites, one company

What is the issue

Both 3sbusinessme.com and 3sbusinessdubai.com are live and indexed by Google at the same time.

How it affects performance

Google splits the ranking power between both sites. Neither site can perform at its full potential - the ranking strength that should belong to one strong brand is being spread thin across two.

Urgent

Slow loading speed and redirect issue

What is the issue

GTmetrix grades the site a C with 68% performance. The Largest Contentful Paint is 2.7 seconds - above Google's acceptable threshold of 2.5s. Google PageSpeed Insights could not even resolve the domain, suggesting a possible HTTP to HTTPS redirect issue.

How it affects performance

A 2.7s load time means visitors may experience a noticeable delay before the page appears, which can affect their confidence in the site. The redirect issue also signals a technical configuration problem that can suppress Google rankings directly.

High

Inner pages missing keyword-targeted titles

What is the issue

The homepage title is well optimised with terms like "Company Formation" and "Business Setup UAE". However inner service pages - free zone, mainland, offshore and PRO services - lack individually targeted page titles with specific search terms.

How it affects performance

Google ranks individual pages not the whole website. Each service page is a separate ranking opportunity. Without specific titles, pages like "Mainland Company Formation Dubai" or "Free Zone Setup UAE" remain invisible to the people searching for exactly that service.

High

Content opportunity not yet explored

What is the issue

There is no blog or content answering common questions business owners search for before contacting a company setup firm.

How it affects performance

Thousands of people search "how to set up a company in Dubai" every month. Competitors are capturing all of that traffic and building trust before the first call is even made.

High

Google reviews not being responded to

What is the issue

3S Business has a strong Google Business Profile with 217 reviews and a 4.9 star rating. However client reviews are not being responded to consistently or professionally.

How it affects performance

Responding to reviews is a direct Google ranking signal for local search. It also shows potential clients that the business is active, attentive and trustworthy. A profile with unanswered reviews loses credibility even when the rating itself is excellent.

Medium

Missing high-value schema markup

What is the issue

The site only has basic Breadcrumb schema. High-value types like LocalBusiness, Service and FAQ schema are missing - even though all this information exists visually on the site.

How it affects performance

Without these, Google cannot display star ratings, service details or FAQ answers directly in search results. Competitors with proper schema markup get enhanced listings that stand out and earn significantly more clicks from the same search position.

Medium

Two profiles causing confusion

What is the issue

There are currently two separate Google profiles appearing for the same address - 3S Business Services L.L.C. (217 reviews) and 3S Group (17 reviews) - both listed at Dubai World Trade Centre.

How it affects performance

When a potential client searches and sees two listings at the same address, it creates confusion about which one to contact. It also splits reviews and authority between two profiles instead of concentrating everything into one strong listing.

Medium

A small piece of draft text is visible on the pricing page

What is the issue

There is a short internal note visible on the live pricing page that reads something like "For best SEO, keep these details as real text." This appears to be a leftover from the website build that was not removed before the page went live.

How it affects performance

It is a very quick fix - but worth flagging as visitors and Google can both see it. Removing it keeps the page looking polished and professional.

The 3-month action plan
Month 1

Technical improvements - Month 1

  • Consolidate both websites into one - redirecting the old domain so all traffic and ranking power flows to the main site.
  • Set up Google Analytics so I can track where visitors come from and what they do once they arrive.
  • Fix the HTTP to HTTPS redirect issue flagged by PageSpeed Insights, compress images and streamline code. Target is GTmetrix grade B or above and LCP under 2.5 seconds by end of month.
  • Update inner service page titles to include specific search terms - the homepage is already optimised but each service page such as Mainland, Free Zone, Offshore and PRO needs its own targeted title like "Mainland Company Formation Dubai" and "Free Zone Setup UAE".
Month 2

Content and visibility - Month 2

  • Maximise the existing Google Business Profile - the 4.9 rating and 217 reviews are a strong foundation. Add weekly posts, updated photos and service-specific content to push into the top 3 local results.
  • Add LocalBusiness, Service and FAQ schema to the website - only basic breadcrumb schema exists currently. This enables star ratings, FAQ previews and rich results to appear directly in Google search.
  • Publish 3 to 4 articles answering real questions like "free zone vs mainland - which is right for my business?"
  • Build dedicated pages for each free zone partner such as DWTC, DIFC and JAFZA to capture separate ranking opportunities.
Month 3

Lead generation - Month 3

  • The WhatsApp button is already present on the site - I recommend upgrading it to open a pre-filled form asking for name, location and reason for enquiry before connecting. This filters conversations and gives the team context before the chat even begins.
  • List the company on top UAE directories including HiDubai, Gulf Business and Clutch to build credibility and improve rankings.
  • I recommend simplifying the contact form to three fields - name, phone and message - to make it as easy as possible for potential clients to reach out.
  • Deliver a performance report covering traffic growth, ranking improvements and leads generated compared to Month 1.

Tools I can build for 3S Group
Below are three tools I can build for 3S Group - practical internal systems accessible from any device and built around how the business actually works. Once paid advertising begins, an additional Ads Performance Dashboard will be provided to give the team a live view of campaign spend, clicks, leads and cost per enquiry - so every dirham invested can be tracked and optimised in real time.

Why a dedicated dashboard?

Many growing businesses find that centralising operations into one system saves significant time and reduces miscommunication. A dedicated dashboard brings everything into one place, gives each team member access to only what they need, and makes tracking progress effortless at any time.

Dashboards I have created
Dashboard 01 Fleet Management Dashboard
▾

A complete vehicle management system that gives operations teams full visibility over their fleet in one screen - tracking renewals, fuel, driver records and fines all in one place.

  • My vehicles - see all company vehicles in one list with current status, renewal dates and alerts so nothing is missed
  • Fuel log - quickly record each refuel with the amount and cost. Monthly totals are calculated automatically
  • Driver list - each driver has a simple profile showing their assigned vehicle and contact details
  • Traffic fines - log a fine against the right driver and track whether it has been paid or is still pending
Dashboard 02 Meeting Minutes Dashboard with Role-Based Access
▾

A structured meeting management tool that keeps every meeting categorised, searchable and visible only to the people who need to see it - based on their role in the company.

  • Categorised minutes - meetings are organised by department, project or topic so finding past decisions takes seconds not hours
  • Role-based access - each employee category sees only the meetings and notes relevant to their level. Senior management sees everything, department heads see their area, and staff see only what applies to them
  • Action item tracking - every action point from a meeting is assigned to a person with a due date, and the dashboard tracks whether it has been completed
  • Search and filter - search by date, keyword, attendee or department to pull up any past meeting in seconds
  • Custom access per company - the access structure can be configured to match the exact employee hierarchy of each 3S Group entity
Dashboard 03 Client Feedback - QR Code to Dashboard System
▾

A QR code is placed at each company location, on invoices, or in follow-up messages. When a client scans it, they are taken to a short feedback form - no apps, no login required. Every response is submitted directly into a centralised dashboard where responsible team members can monitor, track and act on feedback in real time.

  • QR scan to form - client scans the code and fills in a simple feedback or complaint form in seconds
  • Centralised dashboard - all submitted responses appear instantly in one place, visible to the relevant team and management
  • Real-time monitoring - responsible authorities can track open complaints, response times and satisfaction trends at a glance
  • Accountability built in - each submission is logged with date, time and location so nothing slips through
Note

The dashboards above are references only. They are shared to give a clear idea of the type of tools that can be built and customised for 3S Group based on the team's actual needs.

Running ads to a slow or incomplete site wastes budget. The ideal window to start is end of Month 2 or beginning of Month 3, once the organic groundwork is solid.
